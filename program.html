Featured Speakers

Yochai Benkler

Professor at Harvard Law School

Benkler studies commonly based peer-production, and networked economy. The author of Wealth in 2006.

Kilnam Chon

Professor at Korea Institute of Science and Technology

Kilnam is an advocate for the open web and digital environment. He has been named to the Hall of Fame of the Internet Society (ISOC) Internet Hall of Fame.

Soh Yoong Noh

Art Director, Art Center Nabi

As the main center for new media art production in Korea, Noh promotes new developable collaboration and networking for the creative industry of technology and the arts.

Julia Leda

Creative Director, Wikimedia Enterprise

Julia is a political, social democracy and artist based in London. She has been involved in many open source creative projects and has worked with copyright law since the early 2000s.

Lila Trettikov

Executive Director, Wikimedia Foundation

Lila became the Executive Director of the Wikimedia Foundation in 2014. She is responsible for the Wikimedia Foundation's 250 employees in 200 countries and millions of users all over the world.

Ryan Merkley

Chief Executive Officer, Creative Commons

Ryan is leading open source projects at the Mozilla Foundation such as the open source movement.
